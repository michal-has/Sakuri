About Us

Welcome to Sakura Sushi & Sake, where Chef Hiroshi Tanaka's passion for sushi meets Japanese culinary artistry. Our restaurant in the heart of Sakura Springs is a celebration of tradition and innovation.

At Sakura Sushi & Sake, we invite you to experience the authentic flavors of Japan in a serene setting inspired by our cherry blossom garden. Our menu, curated by Chef Hiroshi, offers a blend of traditional favorites and innovative creations, each meticulously crafted using the freshest, highest-quality ingredients.
Join us in our warm and welcoming atmosphere, designed for both seasoned sushi enthusiasts and first-time visitors. Whether you're savoring our signature rolls or indulging in artfully presented sashimi, every bite at Sakura Sushi & Sake tells a story of culinary excellence.
